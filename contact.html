Frequently Asked Questions

How will sessions be conducted?

Sessions will be conducted via Zoom or Google Meet. Face-to-face sessions are also possible in District 7, HCMC for sessions at least 2 hours in length.

How is payment handled?

Payment can be made using Bank Transfer, Paypal or cash for face-to-face sessions.

What materials are required to participate in the tutoring sessions?

There is no set requirement for materials although I prefer you being able to hand-write and share what has been written during the sessions. All responses can be typed but I want you to be prepared for real exam situations. At minimum, a device with strong Internet connection is required. Pen, highlighters and lined paper would prove helpful.

Do you offer trial tutoring sessions?

I'd be happy to offer a 15 minute trial focusing on a collaborative analysis of a mock Paper 1 text - please indicate if you would like to arrange this when you contact me.

What times are tutoring sessions available?

Online sessions - Typically, I am available from 17:00 until 22:00 but from March-May 2024, I should be available from 09:00-22:00. (All times - Vietnam Local Time)

Face-to-face - To arrange based on when we are both available.
